> **Hook:** This app is built BY anglers, FOR anglers. And we want YOU to decide what we add next. 🇮🇪🎣
>
> In the last few months, we've rolled out massive updates like:
> ✅ Live Tides for 30+ stations
> 🗺️ Lough & Sea Depth Charts (corrib, derg, coast contours!)
> 👥 Community spot maps & catch feeds
> 
> But we're only getting started. We want to know exactly what would make your fishing sessions better. No preset selections, no limitations—just pure community ideas. 
> 
> 💬 **What feature do you want to see added to the hub next?**
