1. Introduction & Scope

The Irish Fishing Hub ("we", "our", or "us") is deeply committed to protecting your privacy. This comprehensive Privacy Policy defines exactly how we collect, process, share, and retain your data when you interact with our web application, mobile views, APIs, and associated services (including the V2 AI Identifier and Community Feed).

2. Explicit Data Collection & Usage

2.1 Mandatory Authentication & Profiling Data

Data Points: Email address, cryptographically hashed passwords, usernames, and profile avatars.

Purpose & Justification: Required to establish your unique identity, issue JSON Web Tokens (JWT) for secure session management, provision access to Pro-tier features, and allow cross-device synchronization.

Storage Vendor: Google Firebase Authentication. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

2.2 User-Generated Content & Community Sharing

Data Points: Catch photos, text logs, declared species, bait details, timestamps, reported weights, and user interactions (likes, reports).

Purpose & Justification: This data forms the core of the Community Feed. By uploading it, you explicitly consent to its public display across the application.

Exposed Metadata: When you upload photos to the community feed, Exif data (camera details, embedded GPS) is computationally stripped on the client-side before upload to Firebase Storage to protect your exact "secret" fishing spots unless you manually declare the location.

2.3 AI Machine Vision Processing

Data Points: Images uploaded specifically to the "AI Species Identifier" tool.

Purpose & Justification: Images are transmitted to our backend AI processing model to execute inference and return a species prediction array.

Retention Guarantee: Images uploaded solely for AI identification (and not posted to the community feed) are processed purely in-memory or in ephemeral storage. They are permanently deleted upon completion of the inference task. We do not use your private AI queries to train future machine learning models.

2.4 Financial & Payment Ledger Data

Data Points: Billing email, active subscription status, payment history identifiers.

Purpose & Justification: To manage Pro subscriptions.

Security Isolation: We do NOT collect, store, or transmit your credit card numbers, CVV, or bank details. All financial transactions are handed off entirely to Stripe Inc. via highly secure iframe segregation. Their privacy standards apply to the financial data.

2.5 Telemetry, Analytics & Advertising Profiles

Data Points: IP addresses, browser user-agent strings, device viewport sizing, operating system versions, and page navigation flows.

Purpose & Justification: This telemetry is utilized inherently by secure web servers to return data. It is also utilized by analytics and Google AdSense to classify application health and serve personalized advertising banners.

We leverage industry-standard third-party sub-processors to run the service. We hold them to strict data protection standards:

About Irish Fishing Hub

Irish Fishing Hub is the ultimate online platform for anglers across Ireland. We provide real-time tide charts, marine weather conditions, and precise fishing spot directories spanning both seawater coastal areas and inland freshwater lakes. Whether you're targeting Atlantic Mackerel from a pier or seeking European Seabass, our interactive fishing map gives you the environmental edge.

Our comprehensive database is built on live data from the Irish Marine Institute and community submissions, ensuring you always know exactly when high tide hits and where to find the absolute best fishing locations near you.

App Features

  • Live Tides: Accurate water level telemetry, moon phases, and real-time station graphs.
  • AI Fish Identifier: Upload a photo of your catch and let our custom-trained Artificial Intelligence immediately identify the Irish fish species.
  • Community Catch Feed: Join thousands of local Irish anglers sharing bait tips, catch logs, and real-time success stories.
  • Tackle & Ramp Directory: Locate nearby tackle shops, boat slips, and marine facilities instantly.
