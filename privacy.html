Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

At Irish Fishing Hub, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you access our application and website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our community:

  • Account Information: When you register for our Community or Premium features, we collect your email address, username, and encrypted password.
  • User-Generated Content: Catch photos, descriptions, and location pins you voluntarily share on the Community map.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we may access your device's imprecise location to center the map on nearby tide stations. We do not track your exact movements.
  • Usage Data & Cookies: Standard analytics (via cookies) to understand how you interact with our tide charts and tools, allowing us to improve performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is exclusively used to:

  • Operate the core application functionalities, including auth via Firebase.
  • Process optional subscription payments securely via Stripe.
  • Deliver personalized content, such as saving your favorite tide stations.
  • Identify fish species via our AI tool. Note: Images processed by the AI are not permanently stored unless explicitly saved to your public catch feed.

3. Advertising & Third Parties

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third-party data brokers under any circumstances.

4. Your GDPR / Data Rights

As an EU resident, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data (Right to be Forgotten).
  • Withdraw consent for location tracking or marketing communications at any time.

To exercise these rights, simply email us at privacy@irishfishinghub.com or use the Contact Support page.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption and secure cloud databases (Firebase) to protect your data from unauthorized access.
