About Irish Fishing Hub

Welcome to Irish Fishing Hub, the ultimate digital companion for anglers across Ireland. Whether you are casting a line into the deep Atlantic waters of the West Coast, targeting bass along the sandy shores of the East, or enjoying the tranquility of our inland freshwater lakes, our platform is designed to give you the data you need for a successful session.

Our Mission

Fishing in Ireland is more than just a hobby—it's a tradition, a passion, and a way of life. Our mission is to modernize the Irish angling experience by providing real-time, highly accurate data in one centralized, easy-to-use application. No more guessing the tides or switching between multiple conflicting weather apps.

What We Provide

We aggregate crucial data points specific to the Irish coast and inland waterways:

  • Live Tide Data: Precise high and low tide times, tidal coefficients, and current water levels for hundreds of stations around the coast.
  • Weather & Conditions: Wind speed, direction, atmospheric pressure, and localized forecasts.
  • Community Connections: A live feed where anglers can share their recent catches, tips, and hotspots securely.
  • Tackle Shop Directory: A comprehensive map of local tackle shops to ensure you are always stocked up.

Built by Anglers, For Anglers

Irish Fishing Hub was built from frustration with clunky, outdated tide websites. We wanted a premium, beautifully designed interface that works flawlessly on mobile devices while out on the rocks or standing on the pier. We are continually updating our features based on feedback from the vibrant Irish fishing community.
